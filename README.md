Conclusion
The Super Store business is performing strongly with:
High total sales of 1.57M
Strong profit generation of 175.26K
Excellent contribution from Office Supplies and Technology categories
Customer behavior indicates:
Preference for COD payment mode
Heavy usage of Standard Class shipping
The dashboard provides valuable insights for improving sales strategy, inventory planning, and customer service operations.
Recommendations
Increase focus on high-performing categories like Office Supplies and Technology.
Promote lower-performing sub-categories such as Accessories.
Improve Same Day delivery services to attract more customers.
Encourage customers to use digital payment methods.
Prepare additional inventory during high-sales months like November and December.
